Our Process

Step 1 - Processing

The journey from waste to fashion begins with our waste-processing center. This is the place where the waste that is donated to us is separated into what can be upcycled, and what cannot be upcycled. Once the sorting process is complete, each item is carefully immersed into a disenfecting bath to remove any residue, dried, and cured with ultraviolet (UV) light to eliminate unwanted germs.

Step 1 - Processing

The journey from waste to fashion begins with our waste-processing center. This is the place where the waste that is donated to us is separated into what can be upcycled, and what cannot be upcycled. Once the sorting process is complete, each item is carefully immersed into a disenfecting bath to remove any residue, dried, and cured with ultraviolet (UV) light to eliminate unwanted germs.

Step 2 - Disassembly

In this step of the process, each piece of cleaned trash is further disassembled into parts that will be used for each of the fashion items. For most items, this means cleaning out the inside so that the shell of the part can be directly repurposed for a new fashion product, but for others, this involves cutting the item into pieces which will then be reformed into the desired product.

Step 3 - Reassembly

In this step of the process, each disassembled part is reassembled into the fashion item it is destined to become by the help of our skilled craft team.

Step 4 - Final Touches

Before the fashion product is sold on the market, we apply a coat of paint, polish, and protective coating to ensure that what we sell lives out its purpose.
